# Eklesia

Eklesia is a devotional monitoring app for churches.

Its purpose is to help church members build a consistent devotional life while allowing leaders to view healthy progress without reading private personal reflections.

## Core Principle

Track the habit. Protect the heart.

## Main Idea

Members will:

- Read a devotional
- Write a personal reflection
- Submit the reflection for AI review
- Receive a summary and engagement confirmation
- Build consistency through streaks and progress

Leaders will:

- See devotional consistency
- View overall engagement
- Identify members who may need encouragement
- Not automatically see private reflections

## Privacy

Personal reflections remain private unless the member chooses to share them.

AI may review the reflection to confirm meaningful engagement, but it should not judge the member’s faith or sincerity.
